Income Share Agreement (ISA) Questions

what is an ISA and hoe does it work

An ISA is a contract in which you agree to pay a part of your salary as EMI to us. It is capped at Rs. 150000 which means once you pay off the entire amount through EMIs or directly you won't be charged further. Also you will only be charged if you get a job of 4 LPA or more in the duration of 36 months after the course is completed. Our students are our investments. Therefore, we handpick our students which we feel fit perfectly at Fascoon.

can i see a copy of the ISA

Yes. It is a legal contract between the parties and is therefore presentable to the stakeholders involved.

when do my ISA repeayment start

The ISA repayments start as soon as you get your first paycheck from the job you get after the completion of the course.

what if i get fired or leave a job in between

The monthly EMIs are paused when your salary goes below the threshold of 4 LPA or in a scenario where you are jobless or get fired. The repayments resume once you get another job with the minimum threshold.

what happens if i don't get an offer for 4 LPA

We wont charge you anything until you get job of minimum 4 LPA. Yes, it’s as simple as that.

do i need to submit may document post getting a job

You will be required to send a copy of your offer letter and your payslip.

how do i pay my ISA amount

Your ISA amount shall be paid via cheques or bank transfer after you receive your first salary.
